The lifecycle

From upload to a locked, verifiable PDF — in one place.

eSign MICO360 replaces slow, paper-based sign-off with a fast, accountable digital workflow. Each stage is recorded, and the original file is never altered.

STEP 01

Upload

Add a PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, image, or text file. Everything is converted to PDF automatically — with exact Office formatting when LibreOffice is present. Drag & drop supported.

STEP 02

Route

Send it to specific signatories or a saved signature group, by company, in sequential or parallel order — with per-approver approval types.

STEP 03

Approve

Approvers review and approve or reject. Every decision is stamped with the user, time, and device. Handle many documents at once with bulk actions.

STEP 04

Sign & stamp

Apply a drawn or uploaded signature, or a cryptographic digital certificate, positioned anywhere on the page — and add the company stamp to every page.

STEP 05

Complete

The final signed PDF is generated and locked. Signatures and stamps are baked in; the untouched original always remains available.

STEP 06

Audit

Every event is written to a tamper-evident, hash-chained log you can filter and export to CSV at any time.

Security & trust

Accountability you can prove.

Signing a document is only half the job — eSign MICO360 is built so the record itself holds up.

  • Integrity
    Tamper-evident trail

    Actions are recorded in a hash-chained audit log; alter any entry and the chain breaks, making tampering detectable.

  • Signatures
    Cryptographic option

    Digital signatures use PKCS#7, embedded in the final PDF and verified with SHA-256 — a later edit invalidates the signature.

  • Access
    Roles & permissions

    Every capability is gated by role. Passwords are stored with one-way hashing and sessions use signed tokens with idle auto-logout.

  • Data
    Stays where you put it

    The desktop edition runs a self-contained server and local database — documents never leave the device. Server deployments run on infrastructure you control.
